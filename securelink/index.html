How It Works

Multi-Layer Security Analysis

SecureLink runs every URL through a comprehensive 4-stage analysis pipeline

01

URL Parsing

Deconstructs the URL into protocol, domain, path, and parameters for deep inspection.

02

Pattern Matching

Matches against known phishing patterns, blacklisted domains, and suspicious keyword databases.

03

Heuristic Analysis

Evaluates URL structure, domain age signals, homograph attacks, and typosquatting indicators.

04

Risk Scoring

Combines all checks into a weighted risk score from 0–100 with a clear safety verdict.

What We Detect

SecureLink identifies 6 major categories of URL-based threats

Phishing

Fake login pages designed to steal credentials by mimicking legitimate sites.

Fake domainsLogin spoofingBrand impersonation

Malware

Links that download or execute malicious software on your device.

Executable downloadsDrive-by installExploit kits

Typosquatting

Domains that closely mimic popular brands with slight spelling variations.

Misspelled brandsCharacter swapsLookalike domains

Redirects

URLs that chain multiple redirects to hide the true destination.

URL shortenersChain redirectsHidden destinations

Scareware / Scams

Fake prize pages, urgent warnings, or social engineering traps.

Free prize luresUrgency tacticsFake alerts

Insecure Connections

HTTP links without encryption that expose data to interception.
